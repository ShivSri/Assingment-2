Lobster Mac n Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 Lobster
  • a pound of shrimp
  • 2 tablespoon Flour
  • 1 stick of butter
  • your choice of cheese 2 cup
  • 1 cup of cold milk
  • Olive oil
  • paprika and salt for seasoning
  • 2 cups of Maccoroni
  • 1 large onion

Directions

  1. boil lobster with some salt
  2. Gather the meat form the lobster
  3. Boil the Maccoroni in the same water as the lobster
  4. Dice the large onion
  5. Add butter and olive oil and saute the onions
  6. Add the lobster and Shrimp to the pan
  7. Cook for five mins and let it cool
  8. In the same pan add butter and flour
  9. After the flour dissolves add cold milk
  10. Keep whisking
  11. Add paprika and salt. Mix it well
  12. Add the cheese
  13. Add the shrimp and Lobster to sauce
  14. Pour the sauce onto the Maccoroni
  15. Add some breadcrumbs to the pasta and bake it for 10 minutes at 400 deg.
